Who We Are

Sigmoid is more than just a technical fest - it's a celebration of innovation, creativity, and technological excellence. Founded with the vision of bringing together the brightest minds in technology, we've grown to become one of the most anticipated technical festivals in the region.

Our Mission

To create a platform where technology meets creativity, where students can showcase their talents, learn from industry experts, and push the boundaries of innovation. We believe in fostering an environment that encourages learning, collaboration, and growth.
