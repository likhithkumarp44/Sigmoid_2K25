Registration Guidelines

Pass Validity

Passes are valid for the entire duration of Sigmoid 2k25. Early bird registrations get additional benefits. Passes must be activated within the first day of the event.

Decision of Judges

All decisions made by the judges during competitions and events are final. No correspondence regarding the results will be entertained after the announcement.

Pass Non-Transferable

Passes are strictly non-transferable and must be used only by the registered participant. Any violation will result in immediate cancellation without refund.

Identity Check

Participants must carry valid college ID and government ID at all times. Random verification may be conducted during the event.

Data Protection

Your personal information is protected under our privacy policy. Data collected will be used only for event-related communications and requirements.

Code of Conduct

Participants must maintain professional behavior throughout the event. Any misconduct will result in immediate disqualification and removal from the event.
